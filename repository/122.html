Innovation and Entrepreneurship

In Service to Humanity

We Aim to Positively Impact the Lives of 1 Billion by 2020 

Over 4 billion people live without adequate food, clean water, modern energy and healthcare, education, or dignified jobs. We believe social entrepreneurship can help address these problems, create livelihoods, and restore human dignity. We unite our mission to lift people out of poverty with the Silicon Valley principles of innovation and entrepreneurship.

 

For Social Entrepreneurs

The Global Social Benefit Institute (GSBI®) provides social entrepreneurs with Silicon Valley mentors to prepare them for growth.

Miller Center

Miller Center for Social Entrepreneurship, located at Santa Clara University, accelerates global, innovation-­based entrepreneurship in service to humanity. Home of the Global Social Benefit Institute, Miller Center trains social entrepreneurs, impact investors, and students.
