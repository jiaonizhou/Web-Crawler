Support the Ignatian Center

How you can help: Your support will further our mission to promote and enhance the distinctively Jesuit, Catholic tradition of education at Santa Clara University.

  • A gift to the Thriving Neighbors Initiative will help build capacity for entrepreneurship, expand educational choice, and provide pathways to prosperity and healthy living in the Greater Washington neighborhood of San Jose.
  • A gift to the Arrupe Community-based Learning Program will expand the walls of the classroom by providing real-world opportunities for students to collaborate and engage with neighboring schools and nonprofits.
  • A gift to the Immersion Program will provide opportunities for students to gain a deeper understanding of local and global issues through multi-day experiences.
  • A gift to the Ignatian Spirituality Program will enhance ways of incorporating spiritual exercises into our daily life for SCU students, faculty, and staff.
  • A gift to Bannan Institutes will extend our ability to reach more SCU and greater community members in dialogue around issues of contemporary religious and cultural debate.
