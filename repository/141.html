AiM

  1. Home
  2. University Operations
  3. Business Services
  4. AiM

AiM is our Integrated Workplace Management system. This system manages all service requests, scheduling, space management and more for the University Operations Division. For Campus Customers this system will allow you to enter service requests online and track them through to completion. We also allow users to view space information for campus buildings as well as costs associated to their requested work orders. 

Training

We offer in person training sessions as well as how-to guides for the Customer features in AiM. To get setup with an AiM account email Facilities-CSC@scu.edu. To access the training information check out  our User Guide and Interactive How-To presentations. If you prefer in-person training sign up for an AiM-Drop In session. All of these can be found in the side box on this page!

For any questions or comments please feel free to contact - Facilities-CSC@scu.edu

More About AiM

Asset Management

AiM maintains all Asset Records associated with vital pieces of University equipment including:

  • Boilers
  • Generators
  • Air Conditioners
  • Onity Locks
  • First Aid Kits
  • Vehicles

Each record is noted by a unique identifier and all useful information pertaining to that specific unit can be stored for reference and tracking.

Customer Request

AiM is our campus wide system for customers to enter Service Requests online. This is an easy service that allows the customers to enter and track requests from entry to completion. To request a log in please email Facilities-CSC@scu.edu. Check out this step-by-step guide on How to Enter a New Customer Request.

Preventive Maintenance

AiM allows preventive maintenance work to be scheduled based on a specified frequency to make certain that all equipment are performing at full capacity. Preventive maintenance includes tests, measurements, adjustments, and parts replacement. Preventive maintenance reduces the occurrences of equipment failure and ensures the continuing operation of the university.

Space Management

AiM houses all campus space data and is accessible by all campus customers. It provides helpful information about properties that is easily accessible including:

  • Square Footage
  • Organizational Occupancy
  • Room Usage
  • Contact Information
  • Floor Plans

This information can be translated into space data reports including space usage statistics to ensure sufficient Facility space to accommodate the needs of the students, faculty and staff. Check out this step-by-step guide about how to Access PDF Floorplans in AiM.

Work Order Management

AiM allows all University Operations employees to view a list of their current and past projects and tasks along with their due date. Employees may also enter specific hours worked on each project and the days worked. It is easily accessed online in order to view them when and where ever they may be needed.
