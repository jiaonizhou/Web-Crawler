Welcome To Santa Clara University!

Your ACCESS Card is the official Santa Clara University ID, library card, debit card, and personalized key to hundreds of SCU facilities.  Eliminating the need to carry cash, it is accepted at the bookstore, all campus venues, ticket offices, vending machines, libraries, network printers, laundry machines and copiers. 

ACCESS Cards are issued to all currently enrolled students and active faculty and staff.  Students must be registered for at least one unit to be eligible for an ACCESS Card.  Please click here to find out how to get your first ACCESS Card OR replace a missing ACCESS card.

ACCESS card account information can be found here.  Funding your ACCESS card is easy, just click here to learn more!

  

For a complete list of locations that accept the ACCESS card on campus, please click here.  Off-Campus Merchants can be found here.  

The ACCESS Card Office is located in Benson Memorial Center on the main floor, west lobby, room 106.
