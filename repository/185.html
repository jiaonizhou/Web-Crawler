The Wellness Center

  1. Home
  2. The Wellness Center

The mission of the Santa Clara University Wellness Center is to support the provision of educational and preventative wellness services on our campus. Within the context of a Jesuit Catholic institution, the Wellness Center is committed to fostering a healthy campus environment by empowering individuals to reach their full potential, make informed positive lifestyle choices, and take responsibility for self-care in order to achieve and maintain optimal levels of wellness.

Our Services

The Wellness Center offers a wide variety of services to meet students' needs:

  • Educational Programs & Workshops
  • Peer Health Educator Program
  • Violence Prevention Program
  • Support and Advocacy for Survivors of Sexual Assault, Dating Violence, and Stalking 
  • Online Alcohol and Marijuana Use Assessments
  • BASICS Alcohol Assessment & Feedback Meetings
  • "Potty Talk" Monthly Wellness Newsletter
  • Book, DVD, & Resource Brochure Resource Library
