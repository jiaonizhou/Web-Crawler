Campus Recreation

Intramural Sports

  1. Home
  2. Campus Recreation
  3. Intramural Sports

The Intramural Sports program at Santa Clara University is one of the most popular, with an average of more than 4,500 student participants per year.

Intramural Sports encourages sportsmanship, teamwork, cooperation, physical fitness, and student involvement. There are structured leagues in a variety of activities for beginners to skilled players. Leagues are organized for minimal time commitment. Intramural Sports leagues are open to all current students, faculty, and staff.

For more information, email intramurals@scu.edu or call the Intramural Sports Office at (408) 551-7183.
