Student Programs

  1. Home
  2. Alumni
  3. Connect
  4. Student Programs

You are a student today, but a Bronco for life. Learn how the alumni family can help you while on campus.

Happening Now!

Become a New Student Welcome Program volunteer today to help congratulate recently admitted students.

  • Life After SCU

    Attention Seniors: Stop by Donohoe every week during the spring quarter to gain skills for life after SCU. Learn about BBQing, wine tasting, networking, and more.

  • Student Alumni Council

    The Student Alumni Council is composed of student leaders who strive to connect the student body with the alumni of the University. Get to know the council members a little more.

  • Bagels & Greenbooks

    A quarterly tradition, students flock to Donohoe Alumni House during "Welcome to Finals" week to grab a much needed bagel and greenbooks in preparation for finals. Check the alumni event calendar for an upcoming Bagel & Greenbooks.

  • Bronco Legacy BBQ

    A great opportunity for legacy students and their families to meet classmates who also have strong University ties during Welcome Weekend.

  • Bronco Brawl

    A battle for school spirit supremacy and a $750 grand prize on the line. Do you have what it takes to win the Bronco Brawl?
