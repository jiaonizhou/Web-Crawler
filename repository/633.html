From studying alongside the first female judge in Tanzania to researching food and water insecurity in the coffee-growing regions of northern Nicaragua, the reach of our work builds knowledge and understanding of the world we share.

SCU supports this important research through a range of University grants and funding sources. Our Sponsored Projects Office also helps faculty secure support for their research, from modest stipends to subcontracts and large-scale grants for major projects. The resources of our Global Engagement Office are available to SCU faculty and visiting scholars as they plan for and engage in their work abroad.
