Make an Impact

  1. Home
  2. Giving
  3. Make an Impact

Four out of five SCU students receive some financial aid.

Hundreds of campus programs spanning arts, science, and athletics would not exist without donor support. Help a specific area at SCU and know that you’re making a big difference.

 

 

  • Santa Clara Fund

    Supporting the Santa Clara Fund makes an immediate impact on today’s students and their experience at SCU through financial aid and funding on-campus opportunities, academic programs, and study abroad.

  • Athletics

    For over 100 years, student-athletes at SCU have achieved success in the classroom, in competition, and in the community. Your support of scholarships helps student-athletes continue this record of excellence.

  • Schools and Centers

    There are many ways to support the academic programs and research of our Schools and Centers of Distinction.

  • Scholarships

    Donors who give to a scholarship fund or establish an endowed scholarship help keep an SCU education available to all students, regardless of financial circumstances.

  • Major Projects and Priorities

    Santa Clara University is embarking on a campaign that will make significant and historic changes in the future of our institution.
