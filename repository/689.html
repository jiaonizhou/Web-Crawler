One donor can make a difference at SCU, but when groups of our supporters join together, history is made.

Discover how you can volunteer, help fundraise, or join with donors who share your priorities in supporting SCU.

Alumni

Every alumni gift, to any area of campus, counts toward Santa Clara's participation rate. Alumni participation refers to the number of undergraduate alumni who make an annual gift of any size to their alma mater.  So when it comes to participation, a $20 gift is weighed the same as a $20,000 gift. Last year, gifts of $100 or less added up to $963,651 - underscoring the fact that every gift makes a difference. Participation is one of the critical metrics used to determine national rankings of universities. It also helps leverage financial support from corporations and foundations. Gifts to the Santa Clara Fund impact the largest number of students as they support scholarships, academic programs, international study and immersion trips, and student initiatives. Thank you for your support.

Reunion Giving

Reunion giving supports current students in a number of different areas including the Santa Clara Fund, which provides assistance through scholarships; academic programs, such as research and hands-on learning opportunities; international study abroad and immersion trips; and student initiatives for on-campus organizations to develop new programs. Gifts to any fund or campus program count toward your class campaign.

If your class is celebrating a reunion this year, it is likely that you'll hear from a classmate about the class campaign. Many of your classmates will be making calls, encouraging others to come back to Santa Clara and to consider making a gift in honor of the reunion as well. We encourage you to participate in your class's reunion campaign with a gift to Santa Clara!

Your gift makes it possible for current students to have the same experiences you did as an undergraduate! If you have questions about your class progress or wish to join your class committee, please find further information here.

Faculty/Staff

There are current and upcoming giving programs that need the help and support of faculty and staff in order to succeed. Your support of Santa Clara University helps further impact the students and programs that inspire your hard work. As we ignite the passion in our students to purse every aspect of a full Santa Clara education, we as faculty and staff can make a philanthropic investment in that future.

Parents

Why does Santa Clara need parent support?

Many parents are surprised to hear that tuition, though substantial, only covers 75% of the cost to educate a Santa Clara student. The University relies on income generated from its endowment and contributions from parents, alumni, and friends to make up the difference.

Last fiscal year, 3,043 families donated a total of $4.27 million to the University. Participation by every parent is important and gifts of all levels are appreciated.

How have gifts to the Santa Clara Parents Fund made an impact?

The Santa Clara Parents Fund is the primary giving vehicle enabling parents to support their student's education at SCU. Gifts to The Santa Clara Parents Fund provide critical flexible funding which is invested in undergraduate programs and resources with the greatest need and potential to impact students:

  • Academic programs
  • Service learning, global engagement, and immersion experiences
  • Student initiatives
  • Scholarships

Parent & Family Leadership Council

The Parent & Family Leadership Council is a new philanthropic society of parents, grandparents, and other family members of Santa Clara students who serve as volunteers and ambassadors to the University.

The Council supports the University in three specific ways:

  • Connecting and networking with Santa Clara families through hosting and attending events while helping identify parent volunteers and donors
  • Engaging with University leadership on key issues that affect Santa Clara families and students
  • Inspiring a culture of philanthropy among students and parents

Council members:

  • Invest in the University through an annual 5-figure leadership gift
  • Attend at least one of two annual Council meetings during Welcome and Family Weekends
  • Serve a renewable two-year term

For more information on Parent Giving or to join the Parent & Family Leadership Council, please contact Jen Stirling '06, M.A. '08, director of parent giving, at jstirling@scu.edu or 408-551-6071.

Each Spring hundreds of students gather on campus to show their gratitude and donor appreciation. To learn more about this annual event and view the Sprinksgiving video Sprinksgiving.

Catala Club

Santa Clara University's Catala Club welcomes women of all faiths who share their spirituality through their common awareness of a Christian life. Catala links the past to the present by cherishing the University traditions and goals established by St. Ignatius of Loyola.

The Catala Club is open to women of all ages who believe in a Santa Clara/Jesuit education and have a desire to raise scholarship funds for deserving undergraduate students. As well as fundraising, Catala women foster SCU spirit throughout our community with our events and activities.
