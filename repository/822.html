    Every person has the potential to be even better.

    The CPD offers robust programs in service of diverse communities, responding to their needs and striving to empower current and future leaders.

    Academy of Blended Learning

    The Academy of Blended Learning (ABL) is offered in cooperation with the Diocese of San Jose to the seven K-8 Catholic schools that form the Drexel School Network. The two year program trains teachers in leading blended learning concepts, and how to expertly leverage technology and data analysis to create a strong and sustainable blended learning environment.

    Learn More

    Academy of Church Leadership

    Learn More

    Effective Principal Program

    The Effective Principal Program (EPP) is offered in cooperation with the Diocese of San Jose to principals in Diocese of San Jose Catholic Schools. The 2 year program is designed to emphasize effective leadership practices amongst principals, and provides a combination of cohort collaboration sessions and personal coaching tailored to individual school sites.

    Learn More

    Join us for RISK: A New Collaborative Learning Experience for Catholic High School Teachers. The 2016 Topic is Laudato Si: Cultivating Sustainability in Our Teaching and in Our Communities
