Academic Technology

Academic Technology

  1. Home
  2. Information Services
  3. Academic Technology

Academic Technology closely aligns support functions by combining Media Services, the Technology Help Desk, Technology Support Services, and Technology Training to provide a broad range of technology resources, equipment and services to the campus community, including support for computing, technology integration in the curriculum, classroom technology, graphics, the campus website, and the campus cable TV system

Computing Support

Support for accessing the SCU network and accounts, printing, hardware troubleshooting, setting up your mobile device, and more.

Contact Tech Help Desk

AV / Media Support

Support for media presentations, video recording and streaming, printing posters, equipment checkout, and more.

Contact Media Services

Campus Cable TV

The campus cable TV system offers a movie channel (41.1) and over 100 channels of programming - available in all of the residence halls.

View programming guide

Camino Support

Camino (running Canvas LMS) supports face-to-face, hybrid, and online courses.

Get Camino Help

Support for Faculty

Resources are available to support faculty innovation to enhance teaching and learning.

Explore Teaching support

Technology Training

Software training and support - classes, open labs, consultations, and web help.

View Training Resources
