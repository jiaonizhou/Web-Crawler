Welcome to Technology Training

Technology Training is dedicated to delivering software training and support to Santa Clara students, faculty, and staff. We offer several learning formats including classes, open lab hours, consultations, and web help that are tailored to help users learn software used in teaching, learning, and scholarship.

Visit our training schedule to register for a course today as space is limited. Please contact the individual course instructor if you have questions. As always, our classes are free and open to all SCU students, faculty, and staff.

Productivity Tip of the Month

Restore Deleted Google Calendar Events

Restore both individual and recurring events after you have deleted them. Simply hover over the selected calendar and choose the drop-down menu to the right. Choose the View Trash option. Select the event(s) and choose the Restore Selected Event at the bottom of your screen.
