Get Ready for Liftoff

  1. Attend a T4 Build Workshop

    Workshops are currently being held. Not receiving workshop invites? Email Nic Bertino and we'll get you setup.

  2. Try the New Calendar

    We have a new calendar coming soon with new features and capabilities. Training dates will be announced shortly.

  3. Find Great Images with Widen

    Widen is a Digital Asset Management tool that will help you find gorgeous images and media for your pages. Sessions on using Widen in a variety of capacities are planned for September.

  4. Make Sure You Are Launch-Ready

    Your site should be fully built by December 15, 2015. Here are the monthly milestones:

    • Sep 15th - Jumpcharts done and implemented.
    • Sep 30th - Training for TerminalFOUR, Event Calendar, and Widen completed.
    • Nov 15th - Final review of pages begins.
    • Dec 15th - Final review completed, content is locked. Testing begins
    • Jan 19th - Launch of the new Santa Clara University website.

    Need assistance? Let us know.
